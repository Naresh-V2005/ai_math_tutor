About AI Math Tutor

Project Overview

AI Math Tutor is an intelligent, interactive educational tool that combines computer vision, OCR, and large language models to help students solve and understand algebraic equations.

By simply uploading a photo of a handwritten or printed algebra problem, students receive structured, step-by-step explanations and feedback on common mistakes.

Technology Stack

Python 3.9+Core language
Flask 3.xWeb framework
Gemini 2.5 FlashAI reasoning & vision
EasyOCRText recognition
OpenCVImage preprocessing
SymPySymbolic math solving
ReportLabPDF generation
python-docxWord export
MathJaxLaTeX rendering
